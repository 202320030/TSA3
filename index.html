About

Bloodborne is a singleplayer-focused action role-playing game with some focus on multiplayer feature called invasions with the player fighting for a "Covenant", which are factions within the game.

The player is a traveller from distant lands, searching for Paleblood, a cure for an unknown disease. they encountered by an old man in a wheelchair who gives them a blood transfusion and utters the words "Fear the Old Blood". The player wakes up in the fictional city called Yharnam where most of the population is infected with the Old Blood, which turns people into grotesque beings , devoid of humanity and sanity. The player must find a way to wake up back to reality or continue this nightmare.
